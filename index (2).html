About Pet Simulator 99

Pet Simulator 99 is a fantastical adventure into the whimsical world of digital pet companions. Dive into a realm where imagination knows no bounds, and every pixel is brimming with joyous possibilities!

Pets

Embark on a journey of discovery as you encounter an enchanting array of virtual critters, from the tiniest of pixies to the mightiest of dragons! Each creature is a testament to the boundless creativity of the human mind.

Features

  • Indulge your inner fashionista with a dazzling array of accessories to adorn your beloved companions!
  • Unlock the secrets of the cosmos as you train your pets to execute gravity-defying stunts that defy the very laws of nature!
  • Prepare to be awestruck as you pit your prized pets against one another in a thrilling spectacle of skill and strategy!
  • Embark on a quest of epic proportions as you journey through fantastical realms teeming with mystery and wonder!
